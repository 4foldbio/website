Our mission

Every year, millions of cancer patients receive a late or incorrect diagnosis, leading to invasive, unnecessary treatments that devastate lives and drain healthcare resources.

At 4fold, we are changing that by developing a low-cost, highly accurate diagnostics platform that analyses protein and DNA markers in a single test. We will catch cancers that other tests miss and dramatically reduce false alarms.

Our fully automated, sample-to-answer device will require minimal training, making it simple enough for staff at a GP surgery or pharmacy to use, yet engineered to scale for massive volumes in centralised labs.

We are eliminating the diagnostic errors that cost lives. Our mission is to deliver more accurate answers in an affordable way and save more than 100,000 lives every year.
